Mental health is a state of emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Understanding mental illnesses helps us better support ourselves and those around us.

1. Depression

Symptoms: Persistent sadness, low energy, loss of interest in daily activities, sleep disturbances.

2. Anxiety Disorder

Symptoms: Restlessness, rapid heartbeat, sweating, excessive negative thinking, difficulty sleeping.

3. Bipolar Disorder

Symptoms: Drastic mood swings between high (mania) and low (depression), impulsive behavior.

4. Schizophrenia

Symptoms: Hallucinations, delusions, confusion with reality, social withdrawal, concentration issues.

5. PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder)

Symptoms: Nightmares, flashbacks of trauma, irritability, avoidance of trauma-related situations.

6. OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorder)

Symptoms: Repeated thoughts, compulsive actions such as washing or organizing.

7. Eating Disorders

Symptoms: Refusal to eat (anorexia), binge eating followed by vomiting (bulimia), negative body image.

8. Sleep Disorders

Symptoms: Insomnia, excessive sleeping, sleep disruptions due to stress or anxiety.
